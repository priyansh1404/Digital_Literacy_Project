What is Digital Literacy?

Digital literacy is the ability to find, evaluate, create, and communicate information using digital technologies — safely, ethically, and effectively. It is not just about knowing how to use a device; it is about thinking critically and acting responsibly in a digital world.

Safe Internet Practices
  • ▸Use strong, unique passwords for every account
  • ▸Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) always
  • ▸Think before you click — spot phishing links
  • ▸Keep personal info private — no oversharing
  • ▸Use trusted websites with https:// only
